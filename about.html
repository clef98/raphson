Sources and Details

This website was created with 51.8% CSS, 29.2% HTML5, 15.1% SCSS, and 3.9% JavaScript. The back-end is deployed and managed via Freenom, Vercel, and Git.

The models present on this site are written by information from "Transport Phenomena in Materials Processing" by D.R. Poirier and G.H. Geiger, "Understanding Molecular Simulation" by Daan Frenkel and Berend Smit, and general public online resources. None of our models qualify as intellectual property.
