Created for All, Open to All

    Students in secondary education and university will find our tutorials complementary to our introductory models. The source code is easily accessible and serve as strong references for developing crucial intuition.

  • Academia

    Our models are compoundable and versatile for a variety of academia needs, educational or research orientated. Prospective contributors gain insight from building models from scratch and tap into our resources.

  • Industry

    Highly adjustable boundaries and characterstics aim to expedite products and services in industry for compoundable algorithms and easily integratable source code.
