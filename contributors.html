All are welcome

Raphson is built and expanded through our network of students, researchers, and engineers interested in leveraging their work to the benefit of our global community. Their contributions are deeply appreciated. We appreciate any interest in joining our community; please reach out to our central team at raphson.team@gmail.com and expect a response detailing further instructions within four days. We currently developing the infrastructure to provide permissions to certain users of the site. Contributers will have the choice of which domain for re-use their algorithms with lie in as well as if their name appears.
